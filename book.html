CHECK THE BOOK's DETAILS

Written for a wide range of readers, this book was designed by a group of talented and officially recognized high-school students to deliver Calculus in a novel rigorous yet intuitive way.
Developed to be approachable, essential topics are covered with no prior knowledge needed of the subject. In addition, a wide variety of advanced concepts are included to help the reader consolidate different ideas together.
Exercises are included after major sections to highlight the necessary tools the reader should acquire before proceeding in the text, requiring an appropriate and careful application of what is learned. All topics are supported by intuitive examples and multiple 2D/3D graphs for even single concepts to ensure clear and complete understanding.
Rather than focusing on common Calculus ideas, the book takes a problem-solving approach by providing over 120 problems with varying difficulty levels. These concentrate on developing diverse skills that a student needs to master the subject.
The Calculus of the Circle is an ideal textbook for those who want to experience Calculus in a fresh intuitive approach as well as those who desire a challenging course that sharpens their skills and help them apply the subject in different fields.
