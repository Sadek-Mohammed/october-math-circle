Course Format

All of our materials and assignments will be uploaded on our web portal, our course will be self-paced with a weekly due date that you must follow, there will be no scheduled sessions, but rather weekly office hours where the instructors are going to answer any of your questions and tackle some of the harder problems of the homework.

Communication Method

The main method of communication will be our discord server, which you'll gain access to upon admission. There will be several discord-based activities, such as Problem of the Week (POTW), where we will post a higher than usual difficult problem, to challenge you mentally and have fun! You will also have the chance to discuss with other students and collaborate with them to tackle problems or gain better intuition about things.

Course Material

During the course, you will receive digital versions of our original handouts. The handouts are based on several calculus and real analysis texts and are designed to be on par with MIT Calculus 18.01 Course. The handouts also include some of the author's own original problems and insights, and the assigned problems were chosen carefully to develop intuition and improve problem-solving skills and push the students to their limits.

Course Topics

  • Limits of Functions & Sequences
  • Continuity & Differentiability of Functions
  • Derivatives and Differentiation
  • Applications of Derivatives
  • Indefinite Integration
  • Definite Integration
  • Applications of Integration
  • Taylor Expansion and Infinite Series
  • A Tour Into Differential Equations

Does the Course Suit Me?

This course is for anyone who desires to learn calculus at an honors level university course and be able to perform well in problem-solving-based competitions such as MIT Integration Bee, Stanford Math Tournament, and OMCC - Calculus Contest. The only prerequisites needed for this course are familiarity with trigonometry and the basic skills of algebra and analytic geometry. Try to solve our sample diagnostic test to see how many of the needed skills you have.
